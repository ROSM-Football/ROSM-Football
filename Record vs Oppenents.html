Record against each Opponent

With on overall record of 178-260-24, St. Mary's was not a perennial football power. Although the team won or shared 6 Catholic League Football Titles, they also had long periods where the team struggled. It was quite interesting reading through each season, especially in the early years. The Tribune mentioned the "Royal Oakers" traveling down to Belle Isle to play the Grosse Pointe Catholic teams. Can you imagine taking the road trip in the late 1920's from the Royal Oak? Below is the overall record against each opponent from 1927 - 1984.
